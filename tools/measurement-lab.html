How to read the vernier
Step 1. Read the main-scale cm mark to the left of the vernier zero.
Step 2. Find the vernier division that lines up exactly with a main-scale tick.

How to read the micrometer
Step 1. Read the whole mm on the sleeve (below the datum line).
Step 2. Check if a half-mm mark is visible (above the line). Add 0·50 mm if so.
Step 3. Read the thimble where the datum line crosses it, × 0·01 mm.
