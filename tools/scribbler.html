This is the web version of Scribbler.
Fully featured, free to use, and runs entirely in your browser — no login, no server. Your work auto-saves to this browser on this device between sessions, but it's not synced anywhere. Clearing browser data or switching devices will remove it.
Native Mac / iPad / Windows / Android apps are in the works for persistent, multi-device Scribbling.

To keep your work forever

  • Click Export (↑ in the toolbar) any time → save as PNG or PDF to your device.
  • Exported files are the only permanent copy. Auto-save is a safety net, not a backup.

Shortcuts

  • Tools: P Pen · H Highlighter · E Eraser · V Lasso · L Laser
  • Aids: R Ruler · T Protractor · O Compass
  • Pages: N New · C Clear · PageUp / PageDown navigate
  • Sizes: 1–5 jump · [ / ] step
  • Edit: ⌘/Ctrl+Z Undo · ⌘/Ctrl+⇧+Z Redo · Esc deselect
  • Zoom: ⌘/Ctrl + scroll · Shift+drag rotate handle to snap to 0/30/45/60/90°

Reset notebook?

This will permanently erase all pages and strokes from this device. Make sure you've exported anything you want to keep.
