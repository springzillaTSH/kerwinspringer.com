    (v) Complete the table inserting the tax to be paid by John ($175 000) and Mary ($400 000):

    NamemthpayTAX
    John$175 000(mthpay − 75000) × 0.40 = (175000 − 75000) × 0.40 = 100 000 × 0.40 = $40 000
    Mary$400 000(400000 − 75000) × 0.40 = 325 000 × 0.40 = $130 000

    1 mark each = 2 marks.

    (c)(vi) Draw a flowchart to represent the construct used in the algorithm. (4 marks)

    The flowchart should show:

    1. Start oval.
    2. Process: TAX = 0 (rectangle).
    3. Output: "Enter your monthly pay" (parallelogram).
    4. Input: Read mthpay (parallelogram).
    5. Decision: mthpay > 75000? (diamond).
    6. YES branch: TAX = (mthpay − 75000) × 0.40 (process box).
    7. NO branch: TAX = 0 (process box).
    8. Both branches merge → Output: "TAX", TAX (parallelogram).
    9. Stop oval.

    Marks: 1 Start/Stop ovals; 1 input/output parallelograms; 1 decision diamond with correct condition; 1 process boxes with correct formulas + flow direction = 4 marks.
